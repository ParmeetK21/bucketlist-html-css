Title: Parasailing

Description

My bucket list includes the exhilarating experience of parasailing, which allows you to soar above the water while taking in spectacular sights and enjoying the thrill of being close to the water.

Start Date

15-01-2023

Completion Date

25-08-2023
Details

The adventure was almost unbelievable. I wasn’t even completely prepared for it. I went to the beach with my family and I didn’t know I could actually go for something I always had in my bucket list. It was peaceful to watch the sea from above. I had the opportunity to do it twice, once before sunset and once after.It was a little spooky the second time. It nearly felt like being consumed by the water. They also drove us quite near the sea, and I felt as though I may go under.It was an unforgettable experience.
